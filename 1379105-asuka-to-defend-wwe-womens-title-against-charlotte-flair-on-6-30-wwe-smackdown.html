Asuka To Defend WWE Womens Title Against Charlotte Flair On 6/30 WWE SmackDown

Asuka and Charlotte Flair will clash on the June 30 episode of WWE SmackDown.

On the June 9 episode of WWE SmackDown, Asuka was presented with the new WWE Women’s Championship. She previously defeated Bianca Belair to win the RAW Women’s Championship at WWE Night of Champions, and the SmackDown stars continued to feud over the gold.

On Friday, Flair returned by crashing the championship presentation and challenging Asuka to a title match. Asuka accepted, and Flair stood tall after a brief brawl.

Adam Pearce later confirmed that Flair will face Asuka on the June 30 episode of WWE SmackDown, which will be held at The O2 Arena in London, England.
