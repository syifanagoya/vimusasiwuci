Brooklyn Beckham obtains a new tattoo of his wedding vows as a sign of love for Nicola.
And there is a whole treatise, not a small promise, “I will love you forever.”

Brooklyn Peltz-Beckham, 23, is known for his love of tattoos. He has already managed to accumulate a decent collection of inscriptions and drawings on the body. Even before the wedding with her lover and now wife Nicola Peltz, Brooklyn made several tattoos in her honor. For example, he has her name engraved on the side of his neck, Nikola’s eyes on the back of his neck, and the name of his wife’s grandmother, Gina, whom Peltz called her second mother, is tattooed on one of his arms, and that’s not all.

Almost two months after they officially became husband and wife, Brooklyn decided on a large tattoo on his right forearm as a token of love and respect for the one and only Nicola. He filled his wedding vow in black cursive. He posted the pictures to his personal account, tagging Nikola’s page.

Here are some quotes from there: “Let me start by saying that I cannot describe in words my love for you, just looking at you, I see my future, and it is like a dream. You are my world, and I keep falling in love with you more and more every day.”
