FAQs About Suzy Amis

Who is Suzy Amis?

Suzy Amis is an American former actress, model, and environmental advocate. She is well-known for her roles in films like Titanic and The Usual Suspects. After retiring from acting, she dedicated her time to environmental causes and sustainable living.

What movies is she known for?

She is best known for her roles in Titanic, where she played Lizzy Calvert, and The Usual Suspects. Other notable films include Fandango, Twister, and The Ballad of Little Jo.

Why did she retire from acting?

She retired from acting to focus on her family and environmental advocacy. She wanted to dedicate more time to her children and her passion for sustainability and eco-friendly initiatives.

What environmental initiatives is she involved in?

She co-founded the Red Carpet Green Dress campaign, which promotes eco-friendly fashion on the red carpet. She also started the OMD (One Meal a Day) movement, encouraging people to eat one plant-based meal a day to benefit their health and the planet.

Is she married?

Yes, she is married to renowned film director James Cameron. The couple married in 2000 and have three children together.

What is the OMD movement?

The OMD (One Meal a Day) movement is an initiative founded by Suzy to encourage people to adopt a plant-based diet for at least one meal a day. This movement aims to improve health and reduce the environmental impact of meat consumption.

Did she co-found a school?

Yes, she co-founded MUSE School with her sister Rebecca. MUSE School is known for its focus on sustainability, being the first school in the country to be 100% solar-powered with a 100% organic, plant-based lunch program.
