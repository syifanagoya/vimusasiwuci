Siddharth’s Takkar now streaming in multiple languages on this OTT platform

Published on Jul 7, 2023 11:00 pm IST


Young hero Siddharth was recently seen in the movie Takkar which was directed by Karthik G Krish. The film was originally shot in Tamil. Divyansha Kaushik played the female lead in Takkar, which was also released in Telugu.

Takkar failed to create any impact at the box office, and it received poor reviews. Now the movie is available for streaming in Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, and Kannada languages on Netflix. Abhimanyu Singh, Yogi Babu, Munishkanth, and RJ Vigneshkanth played crucial roles.

Takkar is jointly produced by TG Vishwa Prasad and Abhishek Agarwal under People Media Factory and Abhishek Agarwal Arts in collaboration with Passion Studios. Nivas K Prasanna scored the music, and Vanchinathan Murugesan cranked the camera.
