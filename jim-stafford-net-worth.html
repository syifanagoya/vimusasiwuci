What is Jim Stafford's Net Worth?

Jim Stafford is an American comedian, musician, singer, and songwriter who has a net worth of $8 million. Jim Stafford was born in Winter Haven, Florida in January 1944. He is a self-taught musician who plays guitar, piano, fiddle, banjo, harmonica, and organ. His self-titled debut studio album was released in 1974 and reached #6 on the US Country chart as well as #55 on the Billboard 200 chart and #48 on the Canadian chart. He also released the albums Not Just Another Pretty Foot in 1975 and New Deal in 1993. Stafford's single "Spiders & Snakes" reached #1 in Canada and #3 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

He also had success with the singles "My Girl Bill" and "Wildwood Weed". As an actor he appeared in the films Any Which Way You Can, E.S.P., Kid Colter, Blood Suckers from Outer Space, and Gordy. Stafford also appeared in episodes of the TV series Gemini Man, B.J. and the Bear, Fantasy Island, The Love Boat, and The Smothers Brothers Comedy Hour. From 1978 to 1983 he co-hosted the TV series Nashville on the Road. Since 1990 he has headlined at his theater in Branson, Missouri.
