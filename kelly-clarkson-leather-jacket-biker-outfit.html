The Voice coach channeled her inner rockstar

The Voice coach Kelly Clarkson has switched up her style of late, ditching ditzy floral prints for a rockstar vibe.

The Miss Independent singer shared her latest rock chick outfit on Instagram, pairing a leather jacket with a billowing black dress which gave fans a peak at her strappy lingerie.

Kelly's fans love her style switch-up, commenting: "This jacket is a big YES," and: "Rocking that outfit."

The star kept her makeup lowkey, eschewing heavy eyeshadow and liner normally associated with a rock vibe for nude lips and smudged brown eye makeup.

Kelly has been opting for darker fashion choices for many of her appearances on The Voice, with statement belts and chokers becoming a regular fixture in her wardrobe, along with bold jackets.

The beloved singer has been going through a difficult time in her personal life, following divorcing her ex-husband Brandon Blackstock, who she was married to from 2013 to 2022.

Kelly and Brandon separated in 2020, with their divorce and ensuing legal affairs finalized in 2022, involving settlements on their family home in Montana and custody of their kids River, eight, and Remington, six

That said, Kelly had some good news to share with fans last week, sharing that tickets for her new show in Las Vegas were finally on sale. 

Alongside a promotional clip of her singing in Sin City, she wrote: "Tickets for my exclusive Las Vegas engagement, chemistry…an intimate night with Kelly Clarkson, are on sale now!!  

She continued: "I am so excited for these shows and couldn’t think of a better place to get back on stage than Viva Las Vegas! Y’all can purchase your tickets right now at ticketmaster.com/KellyVegas. I hope to see y’all there. Link in bio."
