Hit Row Arrives On WWE SmackDown

If you didn’t know, now you know; has officially arrived on WWE SmackDown.

NXT’s breakout stable moved to the blue brand in the WWE Draft and made its official debut on the October 22 episode. Isaiah “Swerve” Scott, Ashante “Thee” Adonis, Top Dolla, and B-Fab rapped their way to the ring, with Top Dolla saying he’s worth “top dolla”, “Swerve” warning that he’s your worst nightmare, and B-Fab saying that she’s “fab” every time she’s in the ring.

When the time for talk was over, “Swerve” and Top Dolla defeated some local competitors in a brief tag team match. “Swerve” got the pin after a double-team move.
